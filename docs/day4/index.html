    Project Presentation

    In this class we've gone over how to identify the workflow and how to begin scaling out your application based on that workflow. In this presentation we'd like to hear about what you have been able to accomplish on your project up to this point. So we have time for everyone, this should be a ~5 minute presentation with 4-5 slides (slides not required, there will be whiteboards for drawing diagrams/plots if needed). Let us know:

    • Give a general description of your application and your workflow.
      • Your project description should be a brief high-level explanation for a general audience.
    • What technology are you using (or planning to use) to scale out?
    • What have you done so far?
    • What challenges have you come across, or do you anticipate?
    • If it is available, show a scaling curve.

    Be sure to cover any challenges or questions that came up during this process. We understand this may take some time depending on your application, so do what you can and let us know what you found out!
